About

Croxen Knowledge is a public knowledge base about AI, IT, automation, and personal technology. It documents practical experiments, lessons learned, and guides — written to be useful to non-specialists.

What this is

This is a controlled projection of a private knowledge base. Content is reviewed and explicitly approved before publication. The source of truth is Markdown; the website is generated HTML.

What this is not

This is not a blog in the traditional sense. It is a living record of learning — what works, what doesn't, and why. Raw activity logs and private notes stay private.

Author

Croxen — a pseudonym. The person behind it works in AI and IT management in regional Australia.
